My Passion for Sports & the Outdoors

By Draysen Hatch

When I'm not working or studying, you'll find me either on the field competing in sports or out in nature enjoying the great outdoors. These activities fuel my competitive spirit and give me the peace and adventure I crave.

Outdoor Adventures

The outdoors has always been my sanctuary. There's something incredibly rewarding about disconnecting from technology and immersing yourself in nature. Here are my favorite outdoor pursuits:

  1. Hunting
    • Big game hunting in the fall season
    • Learning tracking and wildlife behavior
    • Conservation and ethical harvesting
    • Building patience and discipline
  2. Fishing
    • Fly fishing in mountain streams
    • Lake fishing for bass and trout
    • Mastering different techniques and lures
    • The thrill of the catch and the peace of the wait
  3. Camping
    • Backpacking into remote wilderness areas
    • Setting up camp and cooking over a fire
    • Stargazing far from city lights
    • Building survival skills and self-reliance

"In every walk with nature, one receives far more than he seeks." - John Muir

Sports & Competition

Sports have taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, perseverance, and pushing beyond my limits. Whether I'm playing or watching, sports bring excitement and community into my life.

Team Sports

Nothing beats the camaraderie of working together toward a common goal. The strategy, communication, and shared victories make team sports incredibly fulfilling.

Individual Competition

Individual sports push me to be accountable only to myself. They build mental toughness and teach me that success comes from personal dedication and hard work.
